Principles

  • Clear hierarchies over clever ones. type scale, monospace numerals, one accent color, lots of nothing.
  • Ship the boring version first. then earn the right to add personality, animation, or copy.
  • Accessibility is not a checklist. contrast, focus order, keyboard parity tested before "design done."
